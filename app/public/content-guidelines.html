Content Guidelines

Creating a Safe and Inspiring Poetry Community

At Shayari wale, we believe in the power of words to inspire, heal, and connect people. To maintain a positive and creative environment for all our users, we have established these content guidelines. By submitting content to our platform, you agree to follow these guidelines.

Our Community Values

  • Creativity: We celebrate original expression and artistic voice
  • Respect: We treat all community members with dignity
  • Inclusivity: We welcome diverse perspectives and languages
  • Authenticity: We value genuine, original content
  • Positivity: We encourage uplifting and constructive sharing

Content We Welcome

✓ Original Shayari and Poetry

Share your own creative works. Original content that expresses your thoughts, feelings, and experiences is always welcome.

✓ Various Themes and Emotions

We embrace diverse themes including love, friendship, motivation, sadness, festivals, and social issues. All emotions have a place in poetry.

✓ Multiple Languages

Submit content in Hindi, Urdu, Tamil, English, or any language you prefer. We celebrate linguistic diversity.

✓ Proper Attribution

If you're sharing someone else's work with permission, always provide proper credit to the original author.

Content Not Allowed

✕ Hate Speech and Discrimination

Content that promotes hatred, discrimination, or violence against individuals or groups based on race, religion, gender, caste, nationality, or any other characteristic is strictly prohibited.

✕ Explicit or Adult Content

Sexually explicit content, nudity, or content intended to arouse is not allowed. Keep your poetry accessible to all age groups.

✕ Violence and Graphic Content

Content that glorifies violence, contains graphic descriptions, or promotes self-harm is not permitted.

✕ Harassment and Bullying

Content targeting individuals with insults, threats, or intimidation is strictly forbidden.

✕ Copyright Violations

Do not submit content that infringes on others' intellectual property rights. Only share original work or properly attributed content with permission.

✕ Spam and Misleading Content

Repetitive content, excessive self-promotion, misleading information, and clickbait are not allowed.

✕ Personal Information

Do not share personal information about yourself or others, including phone numbers, addresses, or private details.

Quality Standards

Formatting Guidelines

  • Use proper line breaks to enhance readability
  • Check spelling and grammar before submitting
  • Use appropriate punctuation
  • Format couplets (sher) clearly with proper spacing

Length Guidelines

  • Short Shayari: 2-4 lines (ideal for quick reads)
  • Medium Shayari: 5-8 lines (balanced expression)
  • Long Shayari: 9-16 lines (detailed expression)
  • Ghazal/Nazm: Multiple couplets with consistent meter

Engagement Guidelines

Liking and Sharing

  • Like content genuinely based on your appreciation
  • Do not use bots or automated tools to generate likes
  • Share content responsibly on social media
  • Always credit the author when sharing outside our platform

Comments and Interaction

  • Provide constructive and respectful feedback
  • Appreciate the effort behind creative works
  • Avoid negative or hurtful criticism
  • Engage in meaningful discussions about poetry

Voucher Program Guidelines

Eligibility Criteria

  • Content must be original and created by you
  • Shayari must receive 500+ genuine likes
  • Likes must come from real users (no bots or fake accounts)
  • Content must comply with all our guidelines
  • Each shayari can only qualify for one voucher

Fraud Prevention

We actively monitor for fraudulent activity including:

  • Artificial inflation of likes through bots
  • Creating multiple accounts to like your own content
  • Coordinated like-farming schemes
  • Submitting plagiarized content

Violations will result in disqualification from the voucher program and possible account suspension.

Content Moderation

Review Process

  • All submissions are reviewed before publication
  • Review typically takes 24-48 hours
  • We may edit formatting for consistency
  • Content that violates guidelines will be rejected

Reporting Content

If you encounter content that violates our guidelines:

  • Use the "Report" button on the content
  • Email us at report@shayariwale.in
  • Provide specific details about the violation

Consequences of Violations

Depending on the severity and frequency of violations, consequences may include:

  • Warning: First-time minor violations
  • Content Removal: Violating content is taken down
  • Temporary Suspension: Account access restricted for a period
  • Permanent Ban: Repeated or severe violations
  • Voucher Disqualification: Removal from reward program

Appeals Process

If you believe your content was wrongly removed or your account was wrongly suspended:

  • Email us at appeals@shayariwale.in
  • Include your username and details of the content
  • Explain why you believe the action was incorrect
  • We will review and respond within 5 business days

Updates to Guidelines

We may update these guidelines periodically to reflect community needs and platform evolution. Significant changes will be communicated to users. Continued use of the platform after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised guidelines.

Contact Us

For questions about these guidelines or to report violations:

  • Email: guidelines@shayariwale.in
  • Report violations: report@shayariwale.in
  • Appeals: appeals@shayariwale.in

Thank you for helping us maintain a creative, respectful, and inspiring community at Shayari wale!
